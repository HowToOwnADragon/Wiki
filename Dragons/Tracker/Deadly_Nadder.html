Spawning

Naturally spawning Deadly Nadders appear in the Overworld. Those within the "Other" category will be explained in a later update. Typically, they appear in groups of a size that ranges from 1 to 4, but sometimes a female Deadly Nadder is able to spawn together with a baby, increasing the size of the group.

The chances for a Female Deadly Nadder to spawn with a baby near her are 1% (1/100). This baby will have the same color as the mother, although the stats might differ.

The gender and variant of wild Deadly Nadders will always be random.

Deadly Nadders will also spawn if you use a Deadly Nadder spawn egg on any block or entity. When the spawn egg is used on a Deadly Nadder, it will spawn a baby.

When a Deadly Nadder spawns, each stat will be randomly generated within a certain range.
The ranges for each stat are as follows:

Health 50-70
Armor 28-34

Walkspeed 0.3-0.5
FlySpeed 0.3-0.5

Behaviour

Deadly Nadders are peaceful dragons that will not attack the player.

When holding 1 of the items used for taming a Deadly Nadder, it will follow the player around.
If the player is absent or not holding taming items and the Deadly Nadder is not tamed, it will wander around aimlessly.

After taming a Deadly Nadder, right-clicking on it with an empty hand will let you ride it.
You can also let it follow you by right-clicking on it with Valka's Staff or by pressing the "All Follow" keybind (G by default).
If you right-click a Deadly Nadder with a Deadly Nadder Saddle, the dragon will equip it. However, as of Alpha 0.0.7 the Deadly Nadder Saddle is completely cosmetic.

Taming & Breeding

In order to tame a Deadly Nadder, you are required to hold one of the following items and right-click on one in the wild.
In Survival Mode, the taming success rate is 30% for each item.
In Creative Mode, the taming is guaranteed.

The items that can be used to tame a Deadly Nadder are:

Cooked Chicken
Raw Mutton
Raw Cod
Raw Salmon

In order to breed Deadly Nadders, Right-click a Male and Female with any of the breeding items in your hand.
Note that you can only breed Deadly Nadders if the weather is clear.

The items that can be used to breed Deadly Nadders are:

Cooked Chicken
Raw Mutton
Raw Cod
Raw Salmon

After Breeding 2 Deadly Nadders, the female will lay an egg.
After the egg is laid, the female will receive a "Breeding Cooldown" of 10 minutes seconds before it is able to breed again.
The male will not receive a cooldown.

To find out how to hatch a Deadly Nadder Egg, check out the Incubator page.

When a Deadly Nadder egg hatches, a Baby Deadly Nadder spawns. For more information on how the inheritance system works, check out "Genetics"

Genetics

When a Deadly Nadder egg hatches, a Baby Deadly Nadder spawns.
The baby will have a 50/50 chance to inherit a color and gender from either parent.
This same chance applies for inheriting stats from it's parents.

The stats that can be inherited are:

Health
Armor
Damage
Walkspeed
Flyspeed
Swimspeed

There is a 1% chance that the stat of the Baby gets mutated, increasing the stat by 1 Point.
The Deadly Nadder can have a maximum of 50 mutations, with a maximum of 20 mutations per stat.

Abilities

The Deadly Nadder has, as of alpha 0.0.7, no abilities. THese will be added later on.

Trivia

The Deadly Nadder was the first ever dragon in the mod to have more than 1 type of drop.
